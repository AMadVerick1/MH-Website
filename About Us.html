About Moripe Holdings

Moripe Holdings is a dynamic, growth-oriented company focused on enterprise development and inclusive economic empowerment. We specialize in business development, training, mentorship, and coaching—delivered with a flexible, mobile approach that enables us to reach clients across South Africa.

Our core mission is to promote job creation and reduce the socio-economic gap by equipping individuals and communities with practical skills and sustainable opportunities. Through our tailored programs, we support entrepreneurs and small businesses at various stages of development.

By fostering innovation, skills development, and economic participation, Moripe Holdings contributes meaningfully to local economic growth and the creation of resilient, thriving communities.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision
