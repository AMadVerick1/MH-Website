Our services

Moripe Consulting

Moripe Holdings offers end-to-end Moripe Consulting services designed to elevate your organization's compliance, performance, and impact. We partner with businesses and institutions to drive transformation through targeted learning interventions that meet both regulatory requirements and strategic workforce objectives. Our services include:

  • Compiling and submitting Workplace Skills Plans (WSPs) and Annual services Reports (ATRs)
  • Assisting with SETA funding applications
  • Aligning services initiatives with the latest QCTO occupational qualifications
  • Developing and managing Learnerships and Internships for meaningful youth employment
  • Facilitating impactful short courses tailored to upskill staff at all levels

With Moripe, Skills Development is not just about ticking boxes — it's about building capability, improving productivity, and creating long-term economic value. We stay current with South Africa's evolving legislative landscape to ensure our clients remain compliant and competitive.

Moripe Financial Services

Moripe Financial Services is dedicated to helping individuals and organizations make informed financial decisions that foster long-term growth and security. We offer a full suite of financial support and consulting services tailored to the needs of our clients, from entry-level entrepreneurs to mature enterprises navigating complex economic landscapes. Our key services include:

  • Business financial planning and advisory
  • Personal finance education and financial literacy workshops
  • Cash flow management and budgeting strategies for SMEs
  • Assistance with tax submissions and compliance
  • Facilitating access to funding through government and private institutions

Whether you're launching a new venture, scaling operations, or managing debt, our financial experts guide you with insights grounded in real-world experience. Moripe Financial Services aims to uplift communities by demystifying finance and creating pathways to sustainable prosperity.

Moripe Distribution

Moripe Distribution strives to become one of the leading distributors of energy in South Africa. Established in 2024 with the newly awarded energy license, the company aims to become an authorised distributor in the Gauteng region striving to meet customers requirements for lubricants specifically for automotive use. Our distribution channels include:

  • Automotive Oil and kits
  • Industrial

Our mission is to provide convinient, high - quality lubricants directly to taxi associations at their primary locations, to enhance their vehicle maintaince on-site. Moripe Distribution strives to be the leading distributor of lubricants in the energy industry.

Our approach is to collaborate with taxi associations or taxi committees to secure permission and establish a formal agreement for an organized operation. In which we will be the main distributors operating in these locations ordering and supplying quality products on a monthly basis.
