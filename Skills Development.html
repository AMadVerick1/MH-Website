Skills Development Facilitation

Moripe Holdings offers end-to-end Skills Development Facilitation (SDF) services designed to elevate your organization's compliance, performance, and impact. We partner with businesses and institutions to drive transformation through targeted learning interventions that meet both regulatory requirements and strategic workforce objectives. Our services include:

  • Compiling and submitting Workplace Skills Plans (WSPs) and Annual Training Reports (ATRs)
  • Assisting with SETA funding applications
  • Aligning training initiatives with the latest QCTO occupational qualifications
  • Developing and managing Learnerships and Internships for meaningful youth employment
  • Facilitating impactful short courses tailored to upskill staff at all levels

With Moripe, Skills Development is not just about ticking boxes — it’s about building capability, improving productivity, and creating long-term economic value. We stay current with South Africa’s evolving legislative landscape to ensure our clients remain compliant and competitive.

Occupational Qualifications (QCTO-Aligned)

We deliver nationally recognized occupational qualifications accredited by the QCTO (Quality Council for Trades and Occupations). These are industry-driven programmes designed to close specific skills gaps in sectors such as Business Administration, Wholesale & Retail, Early Childhood Development, and IT Support. Our qualifications prepare learners for real-world work environments by combining:

  • Structured theoretical instruction
  • Practical workplace experience
  • Summative assessments aligned with occupational standards

We work closely with employers to facilitate workplace placement, monitor learner progress, and ensure successful certification. Whether you're a learner looking to become job-ready or an employer building your future talent pipeline — we're here to bridge that gap.

Short Courses

Our short courses are fast-paced, high-impact interventions designed to deliver practical skills in a compressed timeframe. Ideal for professionals, jobseekers, and businesses aiming to upskill teams without long-term study commitments. Available in areas such as:

  • Entrepreneurship
  • Digital Literacy
  • Project Management
  • Workplace Readiness
  • Financial Life Skills
  • Soft Skills

Each course is outcomes-based and includes practical exercises, case studies, and industry insights — making learning relevant, engaging, and immediately applicable. We also offer custom-designed short courses for organizations wanting to tailor training to specific operational needs.

Learnerships & Internships

At the heart of our mission is a commitment to youth development and sustainable employment. Our learnership and internship programmes are accredited, structured, and aligned to national skills priorities, offering young people the opportunity to gain a qualification while gaining hands-on experience in the workplace. We assist both learners and host employers with:

  • Internship placement
  • Learnership implementation and management
  • Stipend management and SETA reporting
  • Candidate recruitment and onboarding
  • Internship monitoring and evaluation

Whether you're an employer looking to benefit from tax incentives and B-BBEE points or a young person seeking to kickstart your career, Moripe is a trusted bridge between potential and opportunity.

Moripe College

Our College Division is committed to foundational education, academic support, and lifelong learning pathways for both school-going learners and adult learners.

Matric Rewrites & Tutoring

We specialize in preparing learners for NSC (National Senior Certificate) and Amended Senior Certificate (ASC) exams. Our expert facilitators guide students through core subjects with focus on:

  • Content mastery
  • Exam technique
  • Time management
  • Study strategies
  • Academic support
  • Access to past papers, revision guides, and mock assessments

Full-time and part-time learning options are available, including evening and weekend classes for working adults. Adult Education & Training (AET)
Our AET programme supports adults who wish to improve their basic literacy, numeracy, and communication skills. This empowers individuals to:

  • Access better employment opportunities
  • Continue into further education
  • Engage meaningfully in civic and economic life

With flexible schedules and qualified facilitators, AET at Moripe is a second chance that opens first-world opportunities.

Moripe College

Matric Rewrites

Life didn't stop your dreams,neither will we. Our Matric Rewrite programme offers learners a second chance to pass or improve their Grade 12 results. Covering key subjects like Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Life Sciences, Business Studies, and English, this course is designed to reignite confidence, restore purpose, and help you step into your future with pride and qualification in hand.

Tutoring

Whether you're catching up or aiming for the top, our tutoring services are tailored to meet learners at every academic level. We offer focused support in core subjects such as Mathematics, Accounting, Physical and Life Sciences, and English. Delivered in small groups or one-on-one, our approach boosts comprehension, builds self-belief, and turns study time into progress.

Adult Basic Education Training (ABET)

It's never too late to start. Our ABET programme empowers adult learners with foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, communication, and life orientation. Designed for those who missed out on formal education, this course restores dignity and opens doors to employment, further learning, and community participation. We meet you where you are and walk the journey forward, together.

Amended Senior Certificate (ASC)

A fresh start for a new future. The ASC is for adults who want to complete their matric and earn a Senior Certificate that's recognized by Umalusi. Covering core and elective subjects aligned with current national curricula, this course is flexible, supportive, and purpose-driven, perfect for learners seeking new academic and career pathways later in life.
